## Notes

- **OCR variations**: Historical newspapers may show Essondon, Essenden (OCR errors)
- **Hyphenation**: Historical papers often used "Foot-ball", "Grand-stand"
- **Nickname evolution**: "Same Olds" was early nickname; "Bombers" emerged in WWII era due to nearby Essendon Aerodrome
- **16 VFL/AFL premierships**: Equal most with Carlton and Collingwood
