# Hawthorn Hawks Research - Search Queries

**Purpose:** Search terms for finding historical records about Hawthorn Football Club and Glenferrie Oval in Australian archives (Trove, PROV, State Library Victoria, etc.)

**Date Range Focus:** 1902-1991 (VFA/VFL era at Glenferrie Oval and Princes Park)

---

| Year | Event |

# Distinguish from suburb references
"Hawthorn Football Club" OR ("Hawthorn" AND premiership)

# Use nickname for later era
"Hawks" AND Hawthorn AND football
```

### Mayblooms vs Hawks
```
# Pre-1943 (Mayblooms nickname)
