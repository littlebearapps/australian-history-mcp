# Fitzroy Lions Research - Search Queries

**Purpose:** Search terms for finding historical records about Fitzroy Football Club and Brunswick Street Oval in Australian archives (Trove, PROV, State Library Victoria, etc.)

**Date Range Focus:** 1883-1996 (VFA/VFL era, ending with Brisbane merger)

## 5. ARCHIVE SEARCH TIPS

### Trove - Key Newspapers
- **The Argus** (1846-1957) - Melbourne daily
- **The Age** (1854-present)
- **The Herald** (1855-1990)
- **Sporting Globe** (1922-1957) - VFL coverage
- **Fitzroy City Press** - Local Fitzroy newspaper

### Trove - Date Ranges
```
# Early VFA era
dateFrom:1883 dateTo:1896

# VFL golden era (8 premierships)
dateFrom:1897 dateTo:1944

# Post-WWII decline
dateFrom:1945 dateTo:1970

# Final decades
dateFrom:1970 dateTo:1996
```

### PROV - Key Series
```
VPRS 3183 - Town Clerk's Correspondence (Fitzroy Council records)
VPRS 8915 - Valuation Field Books (property records)
VPRS 267 - Supreme Court Civil Case Files
```

## Notes

- **OCR variations**: Historical newspapers may show Filzroy, Fitzrov (OCR errors)
- **Hyphenation**: Historical papers often used "Foot-ball", "Grand-stand"
- **Nickname transitions**: Check era carefully - Maroons (pre-1938), Gorillas (1938-57), Lions (1957+)
- **Ground moves**: Fitzroy played at 4 different grounds over their history
