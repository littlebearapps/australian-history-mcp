## Notes

- **OCR variations**: Historical newspapers may show Geelng, Gelong (OCR errors)
- **Hyphenation**: Historical papers often used "Foot-ball", "Grand-stand"
- **Second oldest club**: Founded 13 April 1859, only weeks after Melbourne FC
- **VFA powerhouse**: 7 VFA premierships in first 9 years (1878-1886)
- **"Pivotonians"**: Old nickname referring to Geelong as "pivot" city between Melbourne and western Victoria
- **Cats nickname (1923)**: Adopted black cat mascot for luck
- **Two grounds**: Corio Oval (1878-1940) then Kardinia Park (1941-present)
- **Navy blue and white hoops**: Said to represent Corio Bay waters and seagulls
- **Geelong Advertiser**: PRIMARY newspaper source for Geelong research
