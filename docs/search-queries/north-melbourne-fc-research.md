# North Melbourne Football Club Research - Search Queries

**Purpose:** Comprehensive search terms for finding historical records about North Melbourne Football Club, Arden Street Oval, and related entities in Australian archives (Trove, PROV, State Library Victoria, etc.)

**Date Range Focus:** Pre-1993 (especially 1869-1985 VFL era at Arden Street)

---

```
# Must contain both terms
"North Melbourne" AND "Arden Street"

# Either term
"Shinboners" OR "Kangaroos"

# Exclude terms
"North Melbourne" NOT "North Melbourne, Tasmania"

# Phrase search
"Arden Street Oval"

# Proximity search (terms within 10 words)
"North Melbourne" NEAR/10 grandstand
```

## Notes

- **Spelling variations**: Historical newspapers often have OCR errors. Try alternate spellings:
  - Melboune, Melbonrne (OCR errors)
  - Ardon, Ardern (OCR errors for Arden)

- **Hyphenation**: Historical newspapers often hyphenated compound words:
  - Foot-ball, foot ball
  - Grand-stand, grand stand
  - Club-rooms, club rooms

- **Date limitations**: Trove newspaper coverage varies. Some titles have gaps or limited digitisation for certain periods.
