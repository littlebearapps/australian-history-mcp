# South Melbourne Swans Research - Search Queries

**Purpose:** Search terms for finding historical records about South Melbourne Football Club and Lake Oval in Australian archives (Trove, PROV, State Library Victoria, etc.)

**Date Range Focus:** 1874-1981 (Victorian era only, pre-Sydney relocation)

## Notes

- **OCR variations**: Historical newspapers may show Sth Melbourne, S. Melbourne (abbreviations)
- **Hyphenation**: Historical papers often used "Foot-ball", "Grand-stand"
- **Emerald Hill**: Historical name for South Melbourne suburb - useful for early records
- **Foreign Legion**: 1933 team recruited heavily from interstate - controversial at the time
- **VFA powerhouse**: 5 VFA premierships (1881, 1885, 1888, 1889, 1890)
- **3 VFL premierships**: 1909, 1918, 1933 (before Sydney move)
- **Victorian research only**: This document focuses on pre-1982 Victorian era
