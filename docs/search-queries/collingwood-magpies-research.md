
## Notes

- **OCR variations**: Historical newspapers may show Collingood, Collingwoood (OCR errors)
- **Hyphenation**: Historical papers often used "Foot-ball", "Grand-stand"
- **"The Machine"**: Collingwood's 1927-1930 team won 4 consecutive premierships - unprecedented
- **16 VFL/AFL premierships**: Equal most with Carlton and Essendon
- **Working-class identity**: Collingwood historically represented Melbourne's working-class inner suburbs
